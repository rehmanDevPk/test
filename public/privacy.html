Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information Collection

When you use PDF Tools Pro, we temporarily store the files you upload solely for the purpose of processing them. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., through a contact form).

2. File Security and Retention

Your privacy is our highest priority. All uploaded files and their processed outputs are automatically permanently deleted from our servers within 2 hours after processing. We do not look at, analyze, or back up your files.

3. Data Encryption

All file transfers are secured using state-of-the-art TLS encryption to ensure your data cannot be intercepted during upload or download.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the functioning of the website. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
